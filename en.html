What is a chocolate fountain?
Chocolate Fountain is a genuine fountain, but the tastiest Belgian chocolate is flowing through it, not water. Like fondue, you can dip pieces of various fruits, biscuits, marshmallows, etc. into the chocolate.
Chocolate Fountain will be a great addition to your wedding banquet, birthday party, corporate party, banquet, or any other celebration and will leave your guests with sweet and unforgettable memories of your holiday.
